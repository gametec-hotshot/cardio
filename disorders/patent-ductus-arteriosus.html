Congenital Heart Disease

Patent Ductus Arteriosus

Failure of ductus arteriosus to close after birth, allowing left-to-right shunting from aorta to pulmonary artery. Common in premature infants.

Prevalence

Rare (in term infants)

Severity

Low-Moderate

Incidence

1 in 2,000 term births, more common in preterm infants

Mortality

Low with appropriate management

Symptoms

dyspnea poor feeding poor growth murmur

Risk Factors

prematurity maternal rubella maternal infection

Diagnostic Tests

echocardiogram chest xray ecg

Treatment Options

indomethacin ibuprofen acetaminophen surgical ligation catheter based closure
